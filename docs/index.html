How it works

60 seconds from install to a live widget on your home screen.

1
Install & open
Download the APK, enable "install unknown apps" in your Android settings, and tap install.
2
Grant permissions
Location (required). Calendar is optional — unlocks premium intelligence. Both are granted once and never asked again.
3
Add the widget
Long-press your home screen, tap Widgets, find WeatherApp. Place it anywhere.
4
Glance and go
The widget updates every 30 minutes automatically. Your only job is to look at it.

Install WeatherApp

This is a direct APK download — not on the Play Store yet. Sideloading takes about 30 seconds.

01
Download the APK from the GitHub releases page below
02
Allow installs — Settings → Apps → Special access → Install unknown apps → your browser
03
Open the APK from your downloads and tap Install
04
Launch and complete the 60-second onboarding
⚠️ This APK is signed with a debug key. Android will warn you it's from an unknown source — that's expected. The app contains no analytics, no ads, and no data collection beyond what's needed to show you the weather.
